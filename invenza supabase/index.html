About Invenza 2026

Invenza 2026 is an ideathon focused on innovation, sustainability, entrepreneurship, and impactful solutions for real-world challenges. Join students from across the country to present transformative ideas that can shape tomorrow.

Innovation Focused

Rules & How It Works

IDEAS FIRST

A working prototype is required to support your pitch. Even a basic functional version is enough.

TIME FOR TEAMS

10-Min Slots
Each team is given a 10-minute slot (6 min pitch + 4 min Q&A).

PRESENTATION STYLE

Use a PPT & prototype demo. A working prototype adds clarity.

No experience needed. No technical background required. If you can think and speak - you can compete. The best ideas come from the most unexpected places. Yours could be next.
